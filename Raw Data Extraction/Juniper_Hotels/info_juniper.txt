About [ edit ]
Incorporated in September 1985, Juniper Hotels Limited is a luxury hotel development and ownership company.[1] The company’s portfolio comprises seven hotels with 1836 keys (including 245 serviced apartments) across strategic locations in India.

Key Points [ edit ]
Property Portfolio[1]
Juniper Hotels operates a luxury hotel chain under the Hyatt brand and owns 19.6% of the Hyatt group-affiliated hotel rooms and apartments in India. Their portfolio includes 8 hotel assets as of FY25, with 2,115 keys

Largest Service Apartments[6] The company has the largest inventory of upper-tier branded serviced apartments in Mumbai and New Delhi among hotels owned by major private investors.

Apartment Portfolio[7] As of FY25, company had 116 serviced apartments at the Grand Hyatt Mumbai Hotel and Residences and 129 serviced apartments at the Hyatt Delhi Residences. In addition to this, the company’s hotels feature an aggregate of 22 renowned restaurants and bars.

Parent Organisation[6] Juniper Hotels is jointly owned by Saraf Hotels and Two Seas Holdings. Two Seas Holdings is an affiliate of Hyatt Hotels Corporation, a well-known international hospitality entity. Through Two Seas Holdings, Hyatt owns half of Juniper Hotels, and JHL is the only hospitality company with a global partner "Hyatt" as an investor.

Revenue Concentration[8]
Co. derives majority of its revenue from Mumbai and Delhi, primarily driven by Grand Hyatt Mumbai and Hyatt Delhi Residences.
It is focusing on diversification through a new acquisition in Bengaluru.

Reducing Debt
Co. reduced its total debt from 2,050 Crs in FY23 to 1,020 Crs in FY25 as the company utilized the majority of its IPO Proceeds for repayment of its high-cost debt. [9]

The company aims to nearly double its key count from 2,115 to ~2,852 keys by FY27, with a further increase to ~2,967 keys by FY28. By FY30, it is targeting significant expansion through multiple growth channels.
