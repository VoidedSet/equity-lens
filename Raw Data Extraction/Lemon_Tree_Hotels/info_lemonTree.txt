Lemon Tree Hotels Ltd

About [ edit ]
Lemon Tree Hotels Ltd is largest mid-priced and the third largest overall hotel chain in India. It operate in the upscale segment and in the mid-priced sector, consisting of the upper-midscale, midscale and economy segments. It delivers differentiated yet superior service offerings, with a value-for-money proposition.[1]

Key Points [ edit ]
Business Profile[1]
As of Q1FY26, Lemon Tree Hotels is a leading Indian hospitality chain, operating 226 hotels with 18,431 rooms across 50+ locations. It manages upscale, midscale, and economy brands, including Aurika, Lemon Tree, and Red Fox, with a growing international presence in Bhutan, Nepal, and Dubai.

The hotels are spread across ~50 locations. Its key hotels are located in Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Ahmedabad, Chandigarh, Jaipur, Kochi, Ludhiana and many more. It also operates hotels in Bhutan, 6 in Nepal, and Dubai.

Managed Hotels
As of Q1FY26, Lemon Tree Hotels manages and franchises 71 hotels with 4,558 rooms under third-party ownership. Additionally, Carnation Hotels Pvt Ltd, its 100% subsidiary, manages/franchises 55 hotels with 3,600+ rooms. It earned Rs. 43.7 Crs as management fees. ~Rs. 25.3 Crs from Fleur Hotels and the rest from franchisee/3rd party.[6]

Group[9]
By FY28, the company plans to transfer all 100% owned assets to Fleur to recycle capital.

1 Fleur Hotels Pvt Ltd - 58.91% subsidiary and the Asset Owner. By/before CY28, the company will be listed via IPO or REIT (Lemon Tree will remain the majority shareholder).

2 Carnation Hotels Pvt Ltd - 100% subsidiary and a Third Party Asset Manager /Franchiser. As of Q3FY25, it manages /franchises 63 hotels, 4087 rooms. By/before CY28, company will manage/franchise 260+ hotels, and 15k+ rooms and propose to merge with Lemon Tree Hotels.

The company plans to merge Carnation Hotels into Lemon Tree Hotels and all 100% owned assets transferred to Fleur to recycle capital. Lemon Tree Hotels will operate & manage /franchise 300+ hotels, with 20k+ rooms.[10]

Focus
The company has a target of 20K+ rooms with a 70%+ Asset light portfolio by 2028. Company is targeting to open 300+ hotels with 65% retail share target by 2028.[12]

Renovation Goals
In Q1FY26, ~350 rooms were under renovation, and the company shall continue to spend on renovation in FY27 and by then the entire portfolio of owned hotels shall be fully renovated and refreshed.[1]

KMP[13]
As a part of the succession planning, promoter Mr. Patanjali Govind Keswani is the new Chairman and Mr. Mr. Neelendra Singh is appointed as the MD of the Company w.e.f. 01.10.2025. Mr. Keswani was the MD for past 22 yrs.
