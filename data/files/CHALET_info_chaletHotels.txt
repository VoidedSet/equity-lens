Parent Group[11] K Raheja Corp, the parent company of Chalet Hotels, is a leading Indian real estate and hospitality conglomerate. It operates across commercial real estate, residential projects, malls, and premium hotels. With over 54 million sq. ft. of leasable space and partnerships with global hotel brands, it emphasizes sustainability and holds a market capitalization of USD 5.49 billion.
